BMI (Body Mass Index) aapka weight (kg) ko height (m²) se divide karke nikalta hai.

Formula: weight(kg) ÷ (height(m) × height(m))

Interpretation (approx): <18.5 = underweight, 18.5–24.9 = normal, 25–29.9 = overweight, ≥30 = obesity.

Note: BMI pure indicator hai — muscular logon ka BMI high ho sakta hai par body-fat kam ho.
BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) woh calories hain jo aapka sharir resting state mein uses karta hai — yani basic body functions ke liye.

Use: Agar aap weight lose ya gain plan kar rahe ho, to total daily calories nikalne mein BMR base hota hai (BMR × activity factor).
Online calculators formula-based approximation dete hain (jaise US Navy, YMCA methods). Ye clinic-level tools (DEXA, hydrostatic) jitne accurate nahi hote.

Tip: Trend dekhne ke liye same method aur same conditions follow karein — isse aapko progress pata chalega.
Protein requirement aapke goal aur activity pe depend karti hai:
  • Sedentary: 0.8 g/kg body weight
  • Active / Strength training: 1.4–2.0 g/kg
  • Weight loss while preserving muscle: 1.6–2.2 g/kg
Calculator estimated value deta hai; medical condition ho to doctor se consult karein.
